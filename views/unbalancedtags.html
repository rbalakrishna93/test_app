Overview

Member Solutions®

At the center of Nationwide’s focus on the member and best serving their needs, Member Solutions has the responsibility to make every customer experience with us as effortless as possible.

Our organization employs nearly 4,500 people across the country who interact with agents and members millions of times a year, on the phone or behind the scenes through processing transactions, billing or shared service support.

Enabled by engaged service professionals, we strive to make it easy for our members to do business with us, to eliminate complexity from our operations, and to think ‘big picture’ so we can solve customer issues before they even arise.

See our 2018 strategic activation slide below, and visit our website to learn more.

Business Units

Business units

Claims Services Claims Services

With more than 600 associates, we deliver on the promises Nationwide makes everyday. Claims Services consists of two main areas: call centers and processing centers. Claims Services handles nearly 1.6 million transactions and 3.1 million calls from agents and customers every year. We exist to serve our members when they need us most.

Commercial and Farm Services Commercial and Farm Services

Our team is made up of three core functions: commercial call centers, commercial processing centers, and collections. Our processing teams handle more than 2 million commercial transactions per year, while our call centers take approximately 700,000 calls from agents and customers annually. Commercial & Farm Services includes more than 1,100 associates. In partnership with our agents, we serve the members who count on Nationwide to protect their farm, ranch or business.

Nationwide Sales Solutions Nationwide Sales Solutions

Established in 2006, Nationwide Sales Solutions (NSS) has quickly become Nationwide's fastest-growing distribution channel, with 573,000 policies in force and $617 million in direct written premium as of Q3 2018. As part of Nationwide Direct, including our Internet sales capabilities, NSS writes business in 46 states (including the District of Columbia) via its toll-free 800 number and in 42 states online.

Direct's product lines include auto, home, dwelling fire, specialty auto, umbrella, powersports, life and commercial coverage. Our One Nationwide suite of products provides a competitive advantage in the industry, as few direct writers can provide a one-stop shop for all personal lines needs.

Personal Lines Services Personal Lines Services

The Personal Lines Services team is made up of two core functions: call centers and processing centers. Our team includes more than 2,000 associates serving members, agents and business partners across the country. We handle more than 8 million service calls and 14.5 million processing transactions annually. When members need support for auto and property insurance, we answer the call.

Shared Services Shared Services

Shared Services originated in 2010 with the goal of improving overall performance and proficiency of key support functions enabling our business operations. Now we are a full-service provider of consultation and work products that lead to coordinated success across multiple business areas. The people, process, and technology solutions we deliver enable associates to be the Nationwide experience while positioning the business to operate more efficiently and effectively.

Our Shared Services disciplines include:

  • Billing & Output
  • Business Technology
  • Change Management & Communications
  • Continuous Improvement
  • Customer Response & Resolution
  • Knowledge Management
  • Quality Assurance
  • Reporting & Analytics
  • Workforce Management

Language Services

Nationwide Language Services

Member Solutions® - Claims Services created Nationwide Language Services to assist associates and agents to better serve our Spanish and Chinese speaking customers. The Spanish teams are located in San Antonio, Texas, and Asian team is based in Columbus, Ohio.

We are Nationwide-trained insurance professionals who specialize in providing language solutions for both oral and written translations between associates, agents and customers. This service is available for all companies and affiliates of Nationwide who have customers who prefer to speak in Spanish or Chinese while conducting business.

You can count on our Language Services team to help you and our members communicate more efficiently through our translation services while receiving an On Your Side® experience.
